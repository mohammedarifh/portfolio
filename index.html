About Me

I am a security professional with over 3.5 years of experience in the field of application security. Currently working at a Big 4 firm, I specialize in identifying vulnerabilities in web applications, LLMs, thick clients, APIs, and mobile platforms & networks (Infra). My journey into security began years ago when my cousin introduced me to hacking tools, sparking a lifelong passion for technology and cybersecurity.

What drives me in this field is the thrill of researching offensive security techniques and the satisfaction of discovering vulnerabilities in seemingly "hackproof" systems. Every time I identify a vulnerability or help secure a complex application, it reinforces my love for what I do. Though I consider myself a slow learner, my approach is to dive deep into the technology, ensuring I understand it thoroughly, which allows me to deliver high-quality results to my clients.

At present, I am focusing on major server-side vulnerabilities and exploring red team strategies. My long-term aspiration is not to become part of core red teaming operations, targeting critical vulnerabilities in highly secure environments.

Outside of work, my passion for security remains so strong that I’m actively trying to explore new, unrelated hobbies! When I’m not immersed in security research, I enjoy learning about world affairs and spending time exploring my inner being, seeking inner emptiness and detachment.

Application Security Specialist @ Big 4.


At Big 4, I have been pivotal in strengthening the security posture of numerous clients by performing in-depth vulnerability assessments and penetration testing. My role involves:


  • Conducting comprehensive security assessments on internal and vendor-facing web applications, APIs, thick clients, and LLMs, evaluating over 150+ security controls based on industry standards, with a combination of manual testing and automated tools.
  • Evaluating security reports generated from project team-run automated scans, including Static Application Security Testing (SAST), Software Composition Analysis (OSS) and Container Security (where applicable), and Dynamic Application Security Testing (DAST), alongside conducting in-depth manual security assessments.
  • Developing custom handy scripts when needed for day-to-day assessments.
  • Reviewing and validating backend configurations against 100+ security controls to ensure secure deployments, adherence to industry security best practices, and compliance with regulatory requirements.
  • Advising project teams on secure development practices, remediation strategies, and ensuring alignment with security policies, compliance standards, and industry best practices.
  • Performing detailed reviews of application architecture diagrams, identifying potential security risks, and providing actionable recommendations to mitigate threats.
  • Researching emerging threats, vulnerabilities, and attack vectors, and implementing proactive security measures to strengthen the overall security posture of each application.
  • Conducting additional checks on policy and compliance controls with security controls to ensure applications meet regulatory and organizational security standards.
  • Delivering Knowledge Transfer (KT) sessions for new team members, providing an in-depth understanding of team processes and offering guidance on specific security and compliance controls. Additionally, presented to the global security team on best practices and procedures for enhancing security initiatives and strengthening security assessments.
